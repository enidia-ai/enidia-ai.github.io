Automated lawyer-client communication

Hybrid AI used for optimizing communication between a lawyer and a client.

Several steps in the lawyer-client communication process can be optimized, such as identifying all the facts and documents relevant to the case, as well as the analysis and computation of all possible legal scenarios. Our advanced automated legal reasoning tools and legal bots enable a seemless integration of these functionalities in your business.

  • Client: Legal firms and customer associations
  • Category: Advanced automated legal reasoning tools

Semantic Search

Enhancing search with domain specific semantic information

Making documents and information accessible is a major challenge to any business. We map and link the data in your documents and make your guides, policies and even legal documents securely accessible via semantic search.

  • Client: Businesses, private and public organizations
  • Category: Knowledge services
