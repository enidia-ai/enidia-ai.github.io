The Registrar

Entity Intelligence & Compliance

Every party in a processed document becomes a structured entity card — automatically, as a side effect of the work you are already doing. The Registrar combines public registry data (RCS, GLEIF, Companies House) with what the document itself says: signing authority, beneficial ownership, obligations. No manual data entry. No integration project. And a complete, sourced verification chain for every entity — exactly what regulators require under AMLD6 and the EU's beneficial ownership framework.
