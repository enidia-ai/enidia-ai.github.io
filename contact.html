About Us

Enidia AI founded in 2021 as a team of academic and industrial experts with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technological innovation and practical legal applications. We are a multidisciplinary team of AI engineers, legal professionals, and academic researchers with deep roots in the AI and law space.

With over 45 years of combined experience, including advanced degrees such as PhDs and LLMs, and long-standing involvement in international AI and law communities like ICAIL and JURIX, we are uniquely positioned to turn advanced research into real-world impact.

Our commitment to ethical AI drives us to develop tools that not only advance legal practice through technology but also uphold the highest standards of reliability, responsibility, and trust.
